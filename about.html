About Us

Who We Are

YaskaTravel is a UK-based travel comparison website dedicated to helping people find great travel deals quickly and easily. We are not a travel agent — we are a free platform that connects you with the world's leading booking services, including Skyscanner, Aviasales, Google Flights, Expedia, and Booking.com.

Our mission is simple: make travel planning easier, faster, and more affordable for everyone. Whether you are a solo adventurer, a family planning a summer holiday, or a business traveller looking for the quickest route, YaskaTravel gives you the tools to find what you need and get on your way.

How It Works

Using YaskaTravel is completely free. Simply enter your departure city, destination, and travel dates into our search panel. With one click, we direct you to the best booking platforms where you can compare prices and complete your booking directly. There are no sign-ups required, no hidden charges, and no booking fees added by us.

We earn a small affiliate commission when you make a booking through one of our partner links. This is how we keep the service free for you, and it comes at absolutely no extra cost — you pay exactly the same price as if you went directly to the booking platform yourself.

Our Values

We believe travel should be accessible to everyone. That is why transparency is at the heart of everything we do. We are honest about how we earn revenue and our goal is to help you compare and explore your options.

We are also passionate about inspiring people to explore the world. Through our travel guides and destination features, we share tips, inspiration, and practical advice to help you get the most out of every journey — whether you are planning your first trip abroad or your fiftieth adventure.
