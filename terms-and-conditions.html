5 ATOL Protection

🛡️ Yasska Ltd holds an Air Travel Organiser's Licence (ATOL) granted by the Civil Aviation Authority (CAA). ATOL number: 12345. This provides financial protection for qualifying package holidays sold by us. When you buy an ATOL-protected flight or holiday from us, you will receive an ATOL Certificate.

ATOL protection covers you if:

  • We are unable to provide the booked package due to insolvency.
  • Your airline fails before your outbound journey.

ATOL protection does not cover all travel products. Standalone flights booked directly with airlines or via third-party platforms are not ATOL protected through Yasska. For more information, visit caa.co.uk/atol.
