Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how information may be collected, used, and protected when this website is used. This website operates as an independent travel comparison platform and may contain affiliate links to third-party booking providers.

Overview

YaskaTravel links users to third-party travel websites where flights, hotels, and related services can be searched, compared, and booked. Bookings are completed on third-party platforms, not on this website.

Information That May Be Collected

Information submitted directly

If a contact form, newsletter form, or email link is used, the information submitted may include a name, email address, and any message content provided voluntarily.

Technical and usage data

Basic technical information may be collected automatically when the website is visited. This may include browser type, device type, pages viewed, referring pages, approximate location, and date or time of access.

Cookies

Cookies and similar technologies may be used to improve website functionality, understand website usage, remember preferences, and support affiliate referral tracking where applicable.

How Information May Be Used

  • To operate and maintain the website
  • To respond to messages or enquiries
  • To improve website performance, content, and user experience
  • To monitor traffic, trends, and technical issues
  • To support affiliate referral tracking and reporting
  • To comply with legal obligations where required

Affiliate Links and Third-Party Websites

This website may contain affiliate links. If a link is clicked and a booking or purchase is completed on a partner website, a commission may be earned at no extra cost to the user.

Third-party travel providers, advertisers, analytics services, and affiliate platforms may collect information under their own privacy policies. Those websites operate independently and should be reviewed directly before any booking or purchase is completed.

Data Sharing

Personal information is not sold. Information may be shared only where necessary with website hosting providers, analytics providers, form handling services, affiliate platforms, or legal authorities where required by law.

Data Retention

Information is retained only for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described in this policy, including responding to enquiries, maintaining records, preventing abuse, and meeting legal or regulatory obligations.

Security

Reasonable steps are taken to protect information, but no website or internet transmission can be guaranteed to be completely secure.

Children's Privacy

This website is not intended for children under 16, and personal information is not knowingly collected from children.

Your Choices

  • Cookies can usually be controlled through browser settings.
  • Marketing emails or newsletters can be unsubscribed from at any time if such messages are offered.
  • Requests relating to personal information can be sent using the website contact details.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always appear on this page with the updated date shown above.

Contact

Questions about this Privacy Policy can be sent through the website contact page or by email where contact details are provided on the website.
