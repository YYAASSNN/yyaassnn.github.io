Cheap Flights to Jeddah: A Complete Guide

Everything you need to know about finding affordable flights to King Abdulaziz International Airport (JED) from the UK and worldwide.

About Jeddah Airport (JED)

King Abdulaziz International Airport is the main gateway for Umrah pilgrims arriving in Saudi Arabia. It sits approximately 85km from Makkah and 19km from Jeddah city centre.

Airport Code
JED — King Abdulaziz Int'l
Distance to Makkah
~85 km (~1 hour)
Distance to City Centre
~19 km
Flight time from London
~6–7 hours direct

Airlines Flying to Jeddah from the UK

Direct Flights

  • Saudi Arabian Airlines (Saudia) — direct from London Heathrow (LHR)
  • flynas — Saudi low-cost carrier with competitive pricing

Connecting Flights (Often Cheaper)

  • Emirates — via Dubai, great from Manchester, Birmingham and Glasgow
  • Qatar Airways — via Doha, highly rated service and comfort
  • Turkish Airlines — via Istanbul, very competitive from UK regional airports
  • EgyptAir — via Cairo, budget-friendly option worth checking
  • Etihad Airways — via Abu Dhabi
💡 Regional Airport Tip

If you live outside London, check Manchester (MAN), Birmingham (BHX), Edinburgh (EDI) and Glasgow (GLA). Connecting flights via Dubai, Doha or Istanbul from these airports can be significantly cheaper than routing through London.

Best Time to Fly to Jeddah

  • Cheapest: January–February and September–November
  • Mid-range: March–June
  • Most expensive: Ramadan, Eid al-Fitr, Eid al-Adha, and UK school holidays

Book 6–10 weeks in advance for the best prices. For Ramadan travel, book 3–4 months ahead.

Top Tips for Finding Cheap Jeddah Flights

  • Compare multiple booking platforms before committing — prices vary for the same flight
  • Be flexible with dates by 1–2 days — small shifts can save £50–£100
  • Consider open-jaw routing — fly into Jeddah, out of Madinah (or vice versa)
  • Search in incognito/private mode to avoid price tracking
  • Set up price alerts to track fare changes over time

Visa Information

UK nationals require an Umrah visa to enter Saudi Arabia for pilgrimage. Apply through an authorised agent at least 2–3 weeks before travel — longer during Ramadan or Hajj season. Your passport must be valid for at least 6 months from your travel date.
