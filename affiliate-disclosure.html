Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: January 2026

YaskaTravel.uk is a free travel comparison platform. This page explains how affiliate links work and what that means for users.

How Commission Is Earned

YaskaTravel.uk participates in affiliate marketing programs. When a partner link is clicked and a booking is completed, a small commission may be earned from the booking platform at no extra cost to the user. The price is generally the same as booking directly.

Partner Platforms

This website links to a range of third-party travel booking platforms, including flight search, hotel booking, travel insurance, and Umrah or Hajj related services.

Does This Affect You?

Affiliate commissions do not affect the prices shown. All bookings are completed on third-party websites, and tickets are not sold directly on this website. Results and links on this website may be influenced by affiliate partnerships.

Contact

Questions about affiliate relationships or this disclosure can be sent to info@yaskatravel.uk.
