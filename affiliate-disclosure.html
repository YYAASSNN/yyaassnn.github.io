Affiliate Disclosure

Last updated: January 2026

YaskaTravel.uk is a free travel comparison platform. This page explains how we earn revenue and what that means for you.

How We Earn Commission

YaskaTravel.uk participates in affiliate marketing programs. When you click a partner link and complete a booking, we may earn a small commission from the booking platform — at no extra cost to you. You always pay the same price as booking directly.

Our Partners

We work with a range of third-party travel booking platforms including flight search, hotel booking, travel insurance, and Umrah & Hajj package providers.

Does This Affect You?

Affiliate commissions do not affect the prices you see. All bookings are completed on third-party websites — we do not sell tickets directly. Results and links on this site may be influenced by affiliate partnerships.
