This Cookie Policy explains how Yasska Ltd uses cookies and similar technologies on yasska.com. It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy. We comply with the Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR) and the UK GDPR.

1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, phone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently, provide a better user experience, and give website owners information about how their site is used.

Similar technologies include local storage, session storage, and web beacons (tracking pixels). For the purposes of this policy, we refer to all of these collectively as "cookies".

🍪 Most web browsers automatically accept cookies. You can choose to block or delete cookies through your browser settings — see Section 6 for how to manage your preferences.

2 How We Use Cookies

We use cookies to:

  • Make our website work correctly and remember your preferences.
  • Understand how visitors use our site so we can improve it.
  • Remember your search preferences (e.g., airports, dates, passenger counts).
  • Track referrals from our affiliate partner links so we can earn commission and keep our service free to use.
  • Show you relevant travel deals and advertisements (where you have consented).
  • Detect and prevent fraud and security risks.

Under PECR, we need your consent to place non-essential cookies on your device. Essential cookies (those strictly necessary for the website to function) do not require your consent, but we still inform you about them.

4 Third-Party Cookies

When you use features on our website that are powered by third parties, those parties may place their own cookies on your device. These are outside our direct control. Key third parties include:

  • Google Analytics — usage and performance analytics (analytics.google.com/analytics)
  • Skyscanner — affiliate flight and hotel search referral tracking
  • Expedia / Booking.com — affiliate booking referral cookies
  • Google Fonts — typography delivery (may log IP addresses)
  • WhatsApp (Meta) — WhatsApp chat widget functionality

We recommend reviewing each third party's own cookie and privacy policies for full details of how they process data.

6 Managing Your Cookies

You have several options for managing cookies:

On our website

When you first visit yasska.com, you will be shown a cookie consent banner allowing you to accept or decline non-essential cookies. You can update your preferences at any time by clicking the "Cookie Settings" link in the footer.

Through your browser

You can control cookies directly through your browser settings. Most browsers allow you to view, block, or delete cookies. Use the links below for guidance on your browser:

Browser Cookie Settings

Find instructions for your browser below. Note that blocking all cookies may affect website functionality.

Opting out of analytics

You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking across all websites by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on available at tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout.

⚠️ Please note: If you delete or block cookies, some features of yasska.com may not function correctly. Your cookie preferences will also be reset if you clear your browser cookies.
