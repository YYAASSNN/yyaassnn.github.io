1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device when you visit a website. They help the site remember information about your visit and are widely used across the internet.

This policy explains the cookies used on yasska.com in line with the UK's Privacy and Electronic Communications Regulations (PECR). It should be read alongside our Privacy Policy.

2 Cookies We Use

We keep our cookie use minimal. Here are the categories in use on yasska.com:

🔒 Essential Always on

Basic cookies needed for the site to function. These include security and session cookies. You cannot opt out of these while using the site.

📊 Analytics Optional

We use Google Analytics to understand aggregate, anonymous traffic data — page views, popular destinations, and traffic sources. No personal data is collected.

🔗 Affiliate Tracking Optional

When you click an affiliate link, our partner platforms (Skyscanner, Aviasales, Expedia, etc.) may set tracking cookies to attribute any commission we earn from your booking.

⚙️ Functional Optional

Cookies that remember your preferences on-site, such as your cookie consent choice, to improve your experience on return visits.

3 Affiliate Tracking Cookies

Yasska is funded through affiliate commissions. When you click a link to Skyscanner, Aviasales, Expedia, Google Flights, or another partner and subsequently make a booking, a tracking cookie records that you came from our site so we receive credit for the referral.

These cookies are placed by the third-party platforms, not by us directly. They do not allow us to see your personal details, booking information, or payment data — only that a referral came from yasska.com.

This is standard practice for affiliate websites and is at no cost to you — you pay the same price as booking directly.

4 Cookies We Don't Use

Because Yasska does not collect personal data or run a booking platform, we do not use:

  • Login or account session cookies (we have no user accounts).
  • Payment or transaction cookies.
  • Advertising profile or retargeting cookies on our own site.
  • Any cookies that collect or store your name, email, or travel details.

6 Managing Your Cookies

You can control cookies in two ways:

On this site: A cookie consent banner appears on your first visit. You can accept or decline non-essential cookies. Your choice is saved for 12 months.

In your browser: You can block or delete cookies at any time through your browser settings. Note that blocking all cookies may affect site functionality:
